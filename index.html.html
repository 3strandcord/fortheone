Our Operating Laws

The "Zero-Leak" Accountability SOP

Establishing absolute legal, banking, and physical money control metrics to bulletproof donor trust.

Dual-Key Control (Segregation of Duties)

No single individual can both authorize fundraising distributions and disburse payments on the ground in Addis Ababa.

  • International Custodian: Manages fundraising allocations securely from abroad.
  • Addis Field Lead: Executes locally verified payments to target schools & landlords.

Zero Physical Cash Policy

To maintain absolute tracing and remove opportunities for receipt manual manipulation, no physical cash changes hands.

  • Direct to Landlord: Transferred directly from mobile wallet to the vendor's phone number.
  • Merchant Code Loops: School fees and medical clinics are settled with registered merchant accounts.

FXD/04/2026 National Compliance

We process international transactions using official National Bank of Ethiopia commercial bank pipelines.

  • Official Bank Inflow: Scanned bank wire confirmations matching daily legal market rates.
  • Auditable Reconciliation: Every dollar spent corresponds directly to a legal digital banking transaction ID.

The One-Month Emergency Buffer

To prevent hasty or unvetted emergency operations, the international custodian holds a strict financial reserve.

  • Stable Security Net: Holds baseline costs (rent and food stasis) ahead of schedule.
  • Secure Request Loop: Addis leads verify emergency medical/housing needs within 24 hours.
